Selected Projects

Below are research and implementation projects I’ve contributed to—spanning community mental health, cancer survivorship, and digital health. Each reflects my commitment to strengths-based and data-informed approaches to advancing equity.

¡Vida! Study

Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center • Jul 2024 – Jul 2025

A SMART trial evaluating culturally tailored lifestyle interventions for Latina breast cancer survivors. I lead participant tracking and tech system integration, co-develop digital materials, and support fidelity monitoring.

  • Developed structured account setup guides and training workflows
  • Integrated Fitbit data with REDCap and Fitabase
  • Contributed to fidelity monitoring and intern training

Manuscript in prep: Curriculum and technology adaptation (Dearden et al., 2025)

ALMA (Amigas Latinas Motivando el Alma)

University of Washington • Jan 2022 – Jul 2022

ALMA is a mental health promotion intervention for Latina immigrant women. I led statistical analyses and dissemination efforts, and mentored master’s students preparing manuscripts.

  • Analyzed survey data using R (ANOVA, chi-square, Kruskal–Wallis, post-hocs)
  • Presented at national conferences and co-authored publications
  • Guided graduate students in translating theses into publications

Publications:

  • CHW-delivered mental health intervention (Frontiers in Public Health, 2025)
  • Participant perspectives on implementation (Journal of Immigrant and Minority Health, 2024)

Under Review: BMC Public Health – Mental health support seeking (Rodriguez et al., 2025)
